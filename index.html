Anokha 2021

Anokha Techfest, the annual event of Amrita School of Engineering, Coimbatore provides an unique opportunity for students to participate in fun-filled and challenging events and workshops. The event throws open opportunities for students from all disciplines to challenge themselves and foster the “Can Do” attitude. Anokha provides the students an invaluable chance to discover, develop and demonstrate their talent, to excel and provides an innovative podium to stand on and succeed. This student-driven techfest showcases and celebrates the innovation, ingenuity, creativity, teamwork, organizational capabilities and talent of the students of AMRITA.

Anokha, the national engineering techfest of Amrita School of Engineering, Coimbatore, is a 3-day congregation of some of the brightest minds in India. Founded in 2010, Anokha has grown by leaps and bounds and has progressed to become one of the most popular and top techfests in India. Having successfully completed ten editions, Anokha has had an average annual participation of over 10,000 of the best undergraduate engineering students from top-ranking engineering institutions in India like IITs, BITS, NITs and IIITs participating as well as partner universities in USA and Europe like University of New Mexico, EVRY France and Uppsala University-Sweden.

Anokha boasts a prize-money of Rs. 15 lakhs with 100+ plus highly competitive contests in all disciplines of engineering, robotics, gaming, business incubation, social media & entrepreneurship, arts, sciences, humanities and visual media. Other highlights of the techfest include fascinating exhibitions & Autoshows, Meet the CEO program, school outreach and techfair showcasing student innovation towards a vision of smart and green campus.

Taking up themes of global importance and societal relevance like Innovation, sustainable development, green technology, Technopolis - smart city and national security, Anokha witnesses 30+ workshops in various cutting-edge areas including its own edition of TEDx-like distinguished talk series called “Lumiere”.
