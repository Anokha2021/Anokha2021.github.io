Vidhan

Vidhan-- the vernacular term for 'Legislature' in India-- is a Youth Parliament event by the Amrita Model United Nations Society. At a time when politics is no longer monopolized by the old and the rich, Vidhan is a space to unleash the Parliamentarian in you. Yes, activism and resistance inspire change - but diplomacy dictates destiny, and only it can effect a change that endures. Vidhan is the perfect platform for all young patriots out there , to unite and deliberate for the sake of India,regardless of their ideaologies.
